Quarterly Operations Summary — Internal Distribution

This report covers the operations team's activity for the most recent quarter.
Overall throughput improved modestly compared with the previous period, driven
mainly by shorter turnaround on routine intake requests and fewer handoffs
between teams.

Highlights:
- Routine request handling time fell by roughly one fifth quarter over quarter.
- The backlog of low-priority items was cleared ahead of the internal target.
- No customer-impacting incidents were recorded during the period.

Areas to watch:
- Onboarding for new team members remains slower than we would like and depends
  on a small number of experienced reviewers.
- Documentation for the intake process is uneven and would benefit from a single
  owner.

Recommendations for next quarter focus on spreading review knowledge across more
of the team and consolidating the intake documentation into one place. None of
the items in this report require escalation or sign-off beyond the team lead.
